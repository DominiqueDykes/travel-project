Why Jamaica?

The Culture, Tradition, and More!

The pull of Jamaica's alluring island is far more than their sparkling blue waters and incredible mountainous terrain. The food is tantalizing and the weather is perfect, but it is the charm of the people who will keep you coming back for more. No matter what hardships may hide beneath the surface, Jamaicans find a way to smile, dance, and be merry as they go about their days.

The history of Jamaica over the past 500 years has been marked by the courage of the Jamaican people in their triumphant struggle for freedom and justice and by their unrelenting resistance and determination in the face of adversity and discrimination.
